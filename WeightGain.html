What is Healthy Weight Gain?

Healthy weight gain involves increasing your body weight through a balanced combination of nutritious foods and regular exercise. It focuses on gaining lean muscle mass rather than excessive fat.

Benefits of Healthy Weight Gain

  • Improved energy levels
  • Increased muscle strength and size
  • Enhanced physical performance
  • Boosted immunity and overall health
  • Improved confidence and body image
